Klonoa Heroes: Densetsu no Star Medal is an action-rpg hybrid featuring the cast and universe from the Klonoa game series.

The story revolves around Klonoa's wish to go out and become a hero. In order to do so he joins the adventurous Guntz who is out on a quest to find a mystery character. Along the way they will find new friends with their own quests, which serve as a way to prove Klonoa's heroism. 

The game is played from a top-down perspective and he is able to transform his magic ring in several weapons. Collecting dream fragments increases Klonoa's experience and upon level-up you can select which stats to increase.
