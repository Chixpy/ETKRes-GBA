Fire Emblem: Fuuin no Tsurugi is the sixth entry in the Fire Emblem series, and the first to come on the GBA, making it the first handled game of it's series.

This games take place 20 years after Fire Emblem: Rekka no Ken (even if it was released before). You take the control of Roy, the son of Eilwood, one of the main protagonists from Rekka no Ken. He's the prince of Pherae, and while going outside with a few of his knights, his castle is being attacked by bandits.

It's gameplay is a lot similar to the previous and following games in the series, you control an army of characters of various classes and fight bad guys on tile-based maps.
